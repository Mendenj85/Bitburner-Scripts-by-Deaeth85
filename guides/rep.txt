rep - Gives a list of ways to earn rep

Methods of gaining rep:
1. Job: higher stats increase rep gain
2. Auguments with reputation bonus (includes Neuroflux Governor)
3. Faction favor, 1 favor = 1% faster gain
4. Donations (unlocked at 150 favor, 1 rep per $1m donated, before bonuses)
5. Infiltrations
6. Coding Contracts can give rep to a single joined faction, or spread across all joined factions
7. Endgame: ||high Intelligence has an effect on reputation gain||
8. Endgame: ||Corps can directly add rep with Corp funds||
